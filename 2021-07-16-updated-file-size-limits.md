# Updated file size limits

Today we released our newest business plan, Enterprise Plus, which supports uploads and downloads up to 150GB. In addition, we upgraded file size limits for some of our existing plans, as detailed below. For details on how to confirm the file size limit for your account, please visit our [upload guide][uploadguide].

## 更新内容

* Enterprise: 50 GB
* Digital Workplace Suite: 50 GB
* Digital Workplace Global Suite: 50 GB
* Digital Business Suite: 50 GB
* Digital Business Global Suite: 50 GB
* Enterprise Plus: 150 GB
